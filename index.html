Lotto Sequence Generator
Click the GENERATE buttons to get a list of potential winning numbers! This generator allows you to set conditions on the sequences generated to slightly increase the chance of winning. The condition values are ordered starting with the most common case, choosing 'Any' for all conditions will generate almost completely random sequences (except the hidden conditions will apply).

Condition Explanations
Pattern: this sequence represents the numbers to pick from pre-determined groups of numbers where the criteria is how many draws ago a certain number appeared. For example, for Lotto Max, a pattern of 1-6-0 means the generated sequences will have 1 number that appeared on the last draw, 6 from 2 to 12 draws ago, and 0 from older draws. For the 6/49, numbers are picked from the last draw, draws 2 to 13 ago, and older draws. For the Daily Grand, numbers are picked from the last 6 draws, draws 7 to 14 ago, and older draws.

Miss 10s: this condition means that a group of tens will not be picked for a given sequence. For example, if the condition is Yes, then there could be no numbers picked from 1 to 9, or 20 to 29. Any group group of tens could be missed.

Even/Odd: this option allows you to choose how many even and odd numbers will appear on a given sequence. For example, 4/3 means there will be 4 even numbers and 3 odd numbers in all the sequences generated.

Hidden: none of the sequences generated will have four or more consecutive numbers picked. Also, none of the sequences will have four or more numbers that have appeared together in more than five previous draws.
